# Q3 2024 Product Strategy
## Executive Summary

Our strategic focus for Q3 2024 centers on **enterprise growth** and **platform reliability**. We are shifting away from consumer-focused features to concentrate on business customers who provide higher LTV and more predictable revenue.

## Key Strategic Priorities

### 1. Enterprise Customer Acquisition (Priority 1)
- Target Fortune 500 companies
- Focus on compliance and security features
- Build admin management capabilities
- Develop enterprise-grade APIs

### 2. Platform Reliability & Scale (Priority 2)
- Achieve 99.9% uptime SLA
- Reduce response times by 30%
- Implement advanced monitoring
- Build disaster recovery capabilities

### 3. Developer Experience (Priority 3)
- Improve API documentation
- Create SDK for major languages
- Build developer portal
- Establish partner ecosystem

## What We're Moving Away From

❌ **Consumer engagement features** - Low conversion rates, high support costs
❌ **Complex UI additions** - User feedback shows preference for simplicity
❌ **Social features** - Not aligned with enterprise focus
❌ **Gaming/gamification** - Doesn't resonate with business users

## Success Metrics

- **Enterprise ARR**: $10M by end of Q3
- **Platform Uptime**: 99.9%
- **Enterprise NPS**: 50+
- **API Adoption**: 500+ enterprise developers

## Resource Allocation

- 60% Engineering: Enterprise features
- 25% Engineering: Platform reliability
- 15% Engineering: Developer experience
- 0% Engineering: Consumer features
